<h2>HexaDash</h2>
HexaDash is a web development project initiated as part of our academy coursework. Our goal was to recreate a website through cloning. However, due to time constraints and the busy schedules of some team members, the project remains incomplete.

<h2>Project Overview</h2>
Name: HexaDash
Type: Web Development
Status: Incomplete
About HexaDash
HexaDash was conceived as an educational endeavor, aimed at honing our web development skills. The project involved the cloning of a website, serving as a practical application of the concepts learned during our coursework.

<h3>Project Status</h3>
Unfortunately, due to various challenges, including time limitations and member availability, we were unable to bring HexaDash to completion. While the project remains unfinished, it stands as a testament to our dedication to learning and exploring new technologies in the field of web development.
